How to share the good news of the Gospel


Step 1
Start conversations around topics that both Christians and non-Christians care about. These can include spiritual questions, personal passions, love, and what the person finds meaningful in life.

Step 2
Give the other person space to talk—and really listen. In my experience, people enjoy sharing about themselves. The goal is to understand them on a deeper level.

Step 3
Look for a natural way to bridge the conversation toward the Gospel.

Step 4
Share the good news of the Gospel using the Three Circles method.
